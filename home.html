Integrated Annual Achievement & Assessment Platform

Streamlining Multiple Annual Assessments into a Single Process & Platform to Reduce Administrative Burden

WHY THE INTEGRATED ASSESSMENT PLATFORM?

The University at Albany has placed a high priority on institutional assessment to implement the campus’s strategic plan, as well as to advance our Carnegie R1 Research University. Currently, there are overlapping requests for annual unit assessments and accomplishments that you are each asked to complete each year. These include:

Overlapping Annual Requests
  • 2020-21 Top 5 Achievements (from President’s Office –> Due May 14) Internal Due Date May 10
  • 2020-21 Major Accomplishments & 2021-22 Action Plan  (from President’s Office –> Due June 1) Internal Due Date May 23
  • 2020-21 Annual Unit Assessment 2021-22 Smart Goals  (from Institutional Research –> Due Date June 30)
  • 2020-21 Division’s Annual Report & Next Year Goals  (from VPR –> due in August)
      Please continue to update your 2021 Annual Assessment and 2022 Planning through August 2021 as needed. 

Reducing Administrative Burden
In an effort to reduce the administrative burden placed on you and your staff, we have developed this integrated platform to streamline these annual assessment processes and to review your results in an easy to access web environment.

How You Benefit
The integrated platform has ingested all of the data/information from the two requests you already completed this year. In all, the platform will eliminate 64 overlapping questions (or 48% of all questions) you would have completed otherwise.
